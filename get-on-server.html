Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a wallet to use OmniTipper?

No, OmniTipper creates a secure wallet for you automatically. Users can start tipping right away without any setup.

Is OmniTipper free to use?

Yes, OmniTipper is completely free. There are no fees on tips, airdrops, or any other transactions.

What permissions does OmniTipper need?

OmniTipper needs basic permissions to read messages, send messages, and manage its own profile in your server.

Can I use OmniTipper on multiple servers?

Yes, once invited, OmniTipper can be added to multiple servers with the same or different configurations.

How do I get support?

Visit our documentation, join our Discord community, or contact our support team for help.

Is my data safe with OmniTipper?

Yes, we use industry-standard security practices to protect your data and transactions.
